Visualizing the Growth of eSports

As an avid video game player and follower myself, I wanted to take a look at the actual growth of the sport after reading numerous articles claiming eSports is being watched more than traditional sports (example). These types of articles generally provided very little data that supported their claims, so I took this project as an opportunity to educate myself and other about the actual growth and popularity of eSports.

On a team with one other member, we collected data from multiple sources, cleaned/organized the data, and visualized it to tell a compelling narrative.
