Gaming + VFX Editing

In my free time, I enjoy editing videos and digital images. I taught myself how to use Sony Vegas Pro and Adobe After Effects a few years ago in order to turn collections of video game clips into synced, aesthetically-pleasing highlight reals. These video game edits are known as fragmovies to the gaming community.

To give each movie a unique look and feel, it was required of me to tweak in-game configurations including lighting, field of vision (fov), sky images, and many other variables.
