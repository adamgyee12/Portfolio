EM2025: Amplifying Mentoring

Project Length: 8 months

Team Members: Maddie Borgmann, Kate Carey, Meg Nidever, Yousef Kazerooni


Research shows that having a mentor provides a world of benefits for children. Our client, The Mentoring Partnership of Southwestern Pennsylvania, has made it their goal to support mentoring programs such that every youth has access to the benefits that a mentoring relationship provides. However, each year nearly 900 kids in the greater Pittsburgh area alone apply for mentors but cannot be served due to limited supply.

Our Solution: EM2025

Through rigorous research, design, and testing, our team has created a framework for the future of mentoring. We call this framework, EM2025.

We believe this future consists of a seamless support structure of caring adults, such that the consistent adults already in a child's life are trained to offer support at a small-interaction level. We call these adults, Everyday Mentors, who can be coaches, teachers, lunch ladies, crossing guards, and the like.

While imagining this future, weâ€™ve designed an end-to-end service that will help adults realize their potential to impact the youth around them, train these adults to be Everyday Mentors, and allow the impact of Everyday Mentoring to be measured.


Stages of EM2025

Implementation of EM2025

To create a conduite in which to reach the goals of EM2025, we designed Magnify, a mobile application focused on building the habit of everyday mentoring in adults. At its core, Magnify delivers curated training material in an interactive method and nudges users to apply the skills learned in their daily lives.

Learn by Doing

Our team crafted a set of training modules that allow users to learn and practice the essential skills of being an everyday mentor. Below is one of 14 training modules, this one focused on recognizing potential impact users can have on the youth around them - one of the core lessons important to learn early on.

Visualizing Impact

Magnify can help create a connected community of Everyday Mentors, rich with stories of mentoring interactions. To help communicate the impact of mentoring, we designed a system that visualizes the stories shared from Magnify and allows the data to become explorable.

Benefits of EM2025

We believe that with our design of an accessible form of Everyday Mentor training that builds awareness and measures impact, a seamless support structure of caring adults can be available for every youth. This is the true benefit of our future vision, and one that we hope to see implemented in the coming years.
